CopyCat Travel App

CopyCat Travel app was created for travelers that feel overwhelmed in planning their upcoming trip itineraries. With too many resources to look through, travelers don’t know which reviews they can trust. CopyCat Travel app solves this problem by connecting users with the trusted travel reviews of their friends. They can see their friends’ trip itineraries, look at their reviews, connect with their friends, and use their suggestions to help the user decide their travel itinerary.
